How it works

Three steps from "let's do a trip" to a booked itinerary.

No back-and-forth emails. No agent retainer. No 40 browser tabs. Just answer a quiz; the rest is done for you.

01 — Quiz

Tell us what you want

~15 questions covering destination, dates, group, budget, course style, dining preferences, transport. Five minutes, no signup required.

02 — Plan

AI builds your trip

A team of agents pulls live flight inventory, matches lodging and tee times, schedules dinners and transfers. You get a complete day-by-day plan with real prices and real availability.

03 — Book

Review and lock in

Swap any item, change a flight, find an alternative. When it's right, hit Book All. Real reservations land in your inbox; hotels and dining settle at the property.
